Recipe

Chocolate cake

"Here is fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate Cake recipe. Enjoy!"

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Black chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Butter: 200g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instructions

1

 Melt the chocolate and butter together

2

 Mix the sugar, eggs and the flour

3

 Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

 Bake in the oven and server in deliciois way with garnishing
